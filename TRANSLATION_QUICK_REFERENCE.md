## What It Does
The Translation Footer is an interactive tool at the bottom of every page that lets students:
- **Type** sentences and get instant translations
- **Speak** sentences and see them transcribed and translated
- **Listen** to correct pronunciations
- **Copy** translations for use elsewhere

## How to Use

### 1. Open the Translator
Click the translation bar at the bottom of the screen to expand it.

### 2. Choose Your Method

#### Method A: Type to Translate
```
1. Type in the left box (English or German)
2. Translation appears automatically in the right box
3. Click speaker icon to hear pronunciation
```

#### Method B: Speak to Translate
```
1. Click the "Speak" button (microphone icon)
2. Speak clearly into your microphone
3. Your speech is transcribed automatically
4. Translation appears in the right box
5. Click speaker icon to hear translation
```

### 3. Additional Features
- **Swap Languages**: Click the â‡„ button to switch source/target languages
- **Copy Translation**: Click "Copy" to copy the translation to clipboard
- **Quick Phrases**: Click pre-made phrases for instant translation
- **Clear**: Remove all text and start over

## Troubleshooting

### "Microphone not working"
- Check browser permissions
- Use Chrome, Safari, or Edge (not Firefox)
- Ensure microphone is connected
- Try refreshing the page

### "Translation not appearing"
- Check internet connection
- Wait a moment (auto-translation has a short delay)
- Try typing more text
- Refresh the page

### "Can't hear pronunciation"
- Check device volume
- Ensure speakers/headphones are connected
- Try a different browser
- Check browser audio permissions
